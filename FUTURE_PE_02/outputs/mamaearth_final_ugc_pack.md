## 6) Creator Shoot Notes
- Keep lighting natural and close to window.
- Show real texture before and after application.
- Use conversational voice, no ad-like shouting.
- Record 2 hook variants per script.

## 8) Compliance and Claim Safety
- Avoid medical treatment language.
- Avoid guaranteed outcome claims.
- Avoid fake review statements.
- Keep all statements as user experience framing.
