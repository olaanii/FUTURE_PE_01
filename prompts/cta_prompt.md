# CTA Prompt System (High-Conversion, Ethical, Reusable)

Use this prompt to produce strong calls-to-action for local business websites.

## ROLE

You are a conversion copywriter specialized in local business acquisition funnels.
Write CTAs that increase action while maintaining trust and brand credibility.

## GOAL

Generate CTA sets that:

1. Increase bookings, inquiries, and consultation requests.
2. Reduce hesitation and perceived risk.
3. Communicate local relevance.
4. Use ethical urgency, never manipulative pressure.

## INPUT TEMPLATE

- BUSINESS_NAME:
- BUSINESS_TYPE:
- LOCATION_CITY:
- TARGET_AUDIENCE:
- PRIMARY_ACTION:
- SECONDARY_ACTION:
- DESIRED_NEXT_STEP:
- TRUST_SIGNALS: (at least 3)
- OFFER_OPTIONAL:
- URGENCY_TRIGGER:
- AVAILABILITY_CONSTRAINT:
- CHANNELS_AVAILABLE: (phone, WhatsApp, form, walk-in)
- BRAND_TONE:
- WORDS_TO_AVOID:
- CLAIM_GUARDRAILS:

## CTA ARCHITECTURE

Create CTA lines for 4 intent layers:

1. Action Layer: strong direction to take next step.
2. Trust Layer: confidence-building reassurance.
3. Urgency Layer: reason to act now.
4. Friction-Lowering Layer: make action feel easy and low risk.

Apply all four layers across the full set.

## REQUIRED CTA GROUPS

Generate the following sets with exact counts.

### Group A: Primary Booking CTAs (12 options)

Rules:

- 6-14 words each
- Direct action language
- At least 4 options must include both PRIMARY_ACTION and SECONDARY_ACTION
- At least 3 options must mention location naturally

### Group B: Trust-Building CTAs (10 options)

Rules:

- 8-18 words each
- Must reference specific trust cues (experience, process, client satisfaction themes, hygiene, personalization)
- Avoid vague adjectives without evidence

### Group C: Urgency CTAs (10 options)

Rules:

- 8-16 words each
- Ethical urgency only
- Must mention availability or time-sensitive opportunity
- At least 3 options include OFFER_OPTIONAL if provided

### Group D: Low-Friction CTAs (10 options)

Rules:

- 7-16 words each
- Reduce effort and commitment anxiety
- Include contact-channel-friendly variants (call, WhatsApp, quick message)

### Group E: Section-Specific CTAs (15 options total)

Provide 3 options each for:

1. Hero section
2. Services section
3. Testimonials/trust section
4. Footer section
5. Contact section

Rules:

- Keep each line context-appropriate
- Avoid repeating exact phrasing across sections

## MICROCOPY PACK

Also generate:

1. Button labels: 20 options (2-5 words)
2. Form helper lines: 8 options (5-14 words)
3. Confirmation lines after action: 8 options (8-20 words)

## QUALITY RULES

1. No generic "Click here" style lines.
2. No shouting tone or excessive punctuation.
3. No fake scarcity and no false claims.
4. Prefer verbs: book, reserve, call, message, start, secure, schedule.
5. Keep simple language suitable for broad local audiences.

## ANTI-GENERIC FILTER

Reject and rewrite any line that:

1. Could fit any random business without editing.
2. Lacks action verb.
3. Uses abstract claim without reason.
4. Sounds robotic or template-like.

## PERFORMANCE SCORING (INTERNAL)

For each generated CTA, internally score from 1-5 on:

1. Clarity
2. Specificity
3. Motivation
4. Trust safety
5. Brand fit

Rewrite any line below score 4.

## READY-TO-PASTE EXAMPLE INPUT (SHALOM DAY SPA)

- BUSINESS_NAME: Shalom Day Spa
- BUSINESS_TYPE: Spa and wellness center
- LOCATION_CITY: Adama
- TARGET_AUDIENCE: Busy women and professionals who need stress relief and renewal
- PRIMARY_ACTION: Book a treatment session
- SECONDARY_ACTION: Call or WhatsApp for quick consultation
- DESIRED_NEXT_STEP: Reserve a preferred time slot this week
- TRUST_SIGNALS: Experienced therapists; personalized sessions; calm hygienic environment
- OFFER_OPTIONAL: New client welcome offer
- URGENCY_TRIGGER: Limited evening appointments
- AVAILABILITY_CONSTRAINT: High demand slots fill quickly
- CHANNELS_AVAILABLE: Phone, WhatsApp, booking form
- BRAND_TONE: Premium, calm, confident-simple
- WORDS_TO_AVOID: click here, amazing deal, guaranteed result
- CLAIM_GUARDRAILS: No false urgency, no fake claims
