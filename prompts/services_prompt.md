# Services Prompt (Persuasive and Benefit-Driven)

You are a conversion-focused website copywriter.

Create service page content that makes local customers trust the business and take action.

## Input Variables

- BUSINESS_NAME: Shalom Day Spa
- LOCATION: Bishoftu
- TARGET_AUDIENCE: Women and professionals who need relaxation and self-care
- BRAND_PROMISE: Personalized premium wellness in a calm environment
- SERVICE_LIST:
  - Massage Therapy
  - Facial Treatments
  - Body Treatments

## Task

For each service in SERVICE_LIST, generate all four elements below.

### 1. Service Name

- Keep concise and client-friendly.

### 2. What You Get

- 25-45 words
- Explain what is included in customer language
- Show immediate and practical value

### 3. Description

- 40-70 words
- Describe emotional + functional outcome
- Connect to real customer pain points
- Keep wording distinct across services

### 4. Why Choose BUSINESS_NAME

- 20-40 words
- Add a concrete trust reason (expertise, personalization, process, environment)
- Avoid empty claims

### 5. Micro-CTA

- One short sentence
- Encourage booking or consultation
- Low friction and action-oriented

## Writing Rules

- Write naturally and clearly; avoid generic filler.
- Do not repeat the same sentence pattern for all services.
- Focus on benefits, outcomes, and customer confidence.
- Keep every section website-ready with minimal editing.

## Output Format

## [Service Name]

What You Get:
[text]

Description:
[text]

Why Choose [BUSINESS_NAME]:
[text]

Micro-CTA:
[text]
