# Homepage Prompt (Conversion-Focused)

You are an expert website copywriter for local businesses.

Write homepage copy that is specific, persuasive, and ready to publish.

## Input Variables

- BUSINESS_NAME: Shalom Day Spa
- LOCATION: Adama
- BUSINESS_TYPE: Spa and wellness center
- TARGET_AUDIENCE: Women and working professionals seeking stress relief
- CORE_PROBLEM: Daily stress, body tension, mental fatigue
- PRIMARY_BENEFIT: Deep relaxation and renewed energy
- UNIQUE_SELLING_POINT: Calm environment with personalized premium care
- TRUST_POINT: Experienced therapists and consistent client satisfaction

## Task

Generate three homepage blocks:

### 1. Headline

- 6-12 words
- Lead with the main benefit
- Must feel emotionally relevant
- Do not start with "Welcome to"

### 2. Sub-headline

- 14-26 words
- Explain who it is for + what result they get
- Mention either LOCATION or BUSINESS_TYPE for local clarity

### 3. Intro

- 60-90 words
- Show transformation from pain point to outcome
- Include one clear differentiation statement
- Include one trust cue
- End with a gentle action-oriented line

## Writing Rules

- Use simple human language; avoid robotic marketing cliches.
- Focus on customer outcomes over service features.
- Keep the tone warm, confident, and realistic.
- Avoid these words unless truly necessary: "unlock", "revolutionary", "game-changing", "best-in-class".
- Keep all claims believable and grounded.
